About

In the 1940s George and Margret opened up a shop that sold and repaired Pocket Watches. An important, vital comodity for individuals during this era.

Their wealth of knowledge have been passed through the genrations in this family run business along with a expansive inventory of Pocket Watch tools and parts

We at Grandpa's Pocket Watch Shop strive to carry on this unique Pocket Watch inductsy by repairing and advising customers, who want to have their Pocket Watch earlooms or collection restored

We also have Pocket Watches to sell that have all different unique histories behind them

Welcome to our Webosite and enjoy the journey!

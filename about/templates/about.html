About Us

In the early 1940s George and Margret opened up a shop that sold and repaired Pocket Watches. Providing these services were an important, vital commodity for individuals during these sparse war years.

Their wealth of knowledge have been passed through the generations along with an expansive inventory of Pocket Watches, Parts, and Tools.

We at Grandpa's Pocket Watch Shop strive to carry on this Pocket Watch business, where we not only sell, but also provide repair and advisory services for customers.

Welcome to our Website and enjoy the visit!
